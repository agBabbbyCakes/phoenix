What is Silverback?

Silverback is a comprehensive bot monitoring and control platform for web3 applications. Track your Ethereum bots in real-time, monitor performance metrics, and maintain complete control over your bot infrastructure—all from one powerful dashboard.

Deploy with Ease

Get your bots up and running in minutes. No complex setup. No cloud dependencies. Just download and launch.

Real-Time Monitoring

Watch your bots perform with live dashboards, latency tracking, success rates, and comprehensive analytics.

Adapt & Optimize

Make data-driven decisions with detailed reports, performance insights, and actionable metrics.

Embed Silverback Widget

Add live bot monitoring to your website or dashboard. Embed your Silverback feed as a widget with just a few lines of code.

How to Embed

▼
  1. 1 Get your Silverback server URL (e.g., https://your-server.com)
  2. 2 Copy the embed code from the options below and paste it into your HTML
  3. 3 Customize the width and height to fit your layout

▼
